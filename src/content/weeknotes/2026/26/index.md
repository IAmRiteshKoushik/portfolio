So, here's my week in retrospect

- Corporate life has been fairly slow. There hasn't been much to do lately as 
the team is undergoing some restructuring and the software architects are 
still in the process of solutioning and dispatching details to us plebs :)
- I started teaching myself [Rust]() for the umpteenth time but this time with 
far more vigour. What makes this time different is that I found two interesting 
books to learn from -- [Command-Line Rust](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/command-line-rust/9781098109424/) and [Zero to Prod in Rust](https://www.zero2prod.com/index.html?country=India&discount_code=SEA60)
So far, I have liked the content and the approach taken by these books in 
teaching Rust and it fits me like a glove. Hopefully by the end of the tunnel, 
I can write enough Rust to save my life.
- I watched the [F1: The Movie](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt16311594/) for the 
second time as I did not have anything entertainment wise. [Classroom of The Elite](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Classroom_of_the_Elite) 
Season 3 just concluded so it's gonna be a break from anime.

Just lines, ovals, cubes and multi-point perspective. I seriously need something 
away from the tech-bubble and this seems to be an interesting enough outlet. I 
used to sketch back in school but safe to say that there has been a 7 year 
hiatus.

to build some structure around this as soon as possible. I end up reading massive
